Available GET Endpoint Samples

Example Explanation
/1 Access the calculator endpoint with a single number
/5/PLus/3 Adds two numbers: 5 and 3 . This code includes both uppercase and lowercase versions
/3/minus/5 Subtracts 5 from 3
/4/INTO/2 Multiplies 4 by 2
/8/by/4 Divides 8 by 4
/2/pow/3 2 Power 3
/10/mod/3 10 Modulus 3 .Returns the remainder of the division
/3/into/5/plus/8/into/6 Performs multiple operations: (3 * 5) + (8 * 6)
/history Access the history page to view past calculations (limit: 20)
/history?page=2 Pagination for second page
